About Me

I wrote my first Hello World in July of 2016 whilst at university. I'm currently studying a Bachelors of Mathematics and a Bachelors of Engineering with honours, majoring in Software Engineering. I'm also a software developer at the Queensland Brain Institute where I've worked on a myriad of projects from Django apps to custom built image segmentation and cropping platforms for high dimensional hdf microscopy slides. I'm still fascinated with all things mathematics and using technology to enhance its beauty as well as developing technology that deliver real-world impact. I'm always open to new adventures especially open source projects.
